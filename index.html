Edge Gateway

SLink — The Site Gateway That Powers SWatch360

SLink is the edge gateway deployed at each branch or site. It connects to supported local security devices, collects health status, buffers data during connectivity interruptions, and sends health events to SWatch360 based on the deployment configuration.

  • Connects to supported cameras, NVRs, fire panels, intrusion systems, access control systems, UPS, and gateways

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does SWatch360 replace our existing cameras or NVRs?
    No. SWatch360 monitors existing infrastructure. SLink connects to supported cameras, NVRs, panels, and gateways without requiring full equipment replacement.
  • Is SWatch360 a video viewing platform?
    No. SWatch360 is focused on device health monitoring, recording visibility, alerts, and compliance-oriented reporting. For video management, SView360 is the complementary platform.
  • What devices does SWatch360 monitor?
    SWatch360 is designed to monitor cameras, NVRs/DVRs, fire alarm systems, intrusion systems, access control systems, UPS units, and SLink gateways, depending on model compatibility and integration configuration.
  • What happens if branch internet connectivity goes down?
    SLink supports store-and-forward behavior, allowing health data to be buffered locally and synced when connectivity is restored, depending on deployment configuration.
  • How does recording visibility work?
    SWatch360 tracks recording status, storage issues, channel availability, and recording gap exceptions where supported by the connected device.
  • Can field engineers access SWatch360 on mobile?
    SWatch360 is browser-based and can be accessed from mobile devices where enabled. Role-based access ensures users see only permitted sites and functions.
  • How long does branch deployment take?
    Deployment time depends on device count, network readiness, and customer IT approvals. A typical branch can be commissioned in phases after site survey and device inventory validation.
  • Can SWatch360 work alongside SView360?
    Yes. SWatch360 handles device health monitoring, while SView360 handles video management. They can be deployed independently or together.
  • Is multi-tenant support available for integrators?
    Yes. Partner deployments can support multiple customers with separated views, branding options, and customer-wise operations.
  • Where is the data hosted?
    Cloud and hybrid deployments can be hosted in India. Private and on-prem deployment options are available on request for restricted environments.
