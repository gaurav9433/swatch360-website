FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does SWatch360 replace our existing devices?
    No. SWatch360 monitors existing infrastructure. SLink connects to supported cameras, NVRs, panels, UPS units, gateways, time-lock systems, and other infrastructure devices without requiring full equipment replacement.
  • Is SWatch360 a video viewing platform?
    No. SWatch360 is focused on device health monitoring, fault alerts, recording visibility, service tracking, and operational reporting. For video management, SView360 is the complementary platform.
  • What devices does SWatch360 monitor?
    SWatch360 is designed to monitor cameras, NVRs/DVRs, fire alarm systems, intrusion systems, access control systems, UPS units, time-lock systems, gateways, and other supported infrastructure devices depending on model compatibility and integration configuration.
  • What happens if site internet connectivity goes down?
    SLink supports store-and-forward behavior, allowing health data to be buffered locally and synced when connectivity is restored, depending on deployment configuration.
  • How does recording visibility work?
    SWatch360 tracks recording status, storage issues, channel availability, and recording gap exceptions where supported by the connected device.
  • Can field engineers access SWatch360 on mobile?
    SWatch360 is browser-based and can be accessed from mobile devices where enabled. Role-based access ensures users see only permitted sites and functions.
  • How long does site deployment take?
    Deployment time depends on device count, network readiness, customer IT approvals, and integration scope. A typical site can be commissioned in phases after site survey and device inventory validation.
  • Can SWatch360 work alongside SView360?
    Yes. SWatch360 handles device and infrastructure health monitoring, while SView360 handles video management. They can be deployed independently or together.
  • Is multi-tenant support available for integrators and facility managers?
    Yes. Partner deployments can support multiple customers with separated views, branding options, and customer-wise operations.
  • Where is the data hosted?
    Cloud and hybrid deployments can be hosted in India. Private and on-prem deployment options are available on request for restricted environments.
